How we protect your privacy

{{if .Brand.Name}}{{.Brand.Name}}{{else}}Jive Search{{end}} is a search engine that doesn't track you. We don't store your personal information; that means we don't store your search terms, ip address nor information about your browser. Your search queries are your business and only your business! Unlike other search engines, we don't pass your search term to the links you click on and we don't follow you around the web with targeted ads. Because of this, you can break out of the filter bubble and get the search results you need, and not the search results someone else wants you to see.

Open Source means greater privacy!

{{if .Brand.Name}}{{.Brand.Name}}{{else}}Jive Search{{end}} is 100% open source, meaning you can view our code and even run it yourself. Any search engine can claim they don't track their users, but how do you really know? Our commitment to open source sets us apart from other major search engines. If your current search engine is not open source, ask yourself, "What are they hiding?".
